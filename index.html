Nurturing Gaza's children with love

In the heart of every child lies the potential to change the world. Despite the challenges and hardships faced by the children of Gaza, there is hope. By providing support, education, and love, we can help nurture their dreams and build a brighter future. Every act of kindness, every donation, and every moment of care contributes to healing and empowering these young hearts. Join us in our mission to nurture Gaza's children with love, and let’s make a lasting difference, together

WAYS TO HELP

WHAT WE DO

We work in the hardest-to-reach places, where it’s toughest to be a child.

When you give to Save the Children, 86% of every dollar goes straight to our mission to provide lifesaving relief to children. $50 can provide enough food to keep 3 children from going hungry for a month. $150 can wrap warm, cozy blankets around 30 children affected by conflict. $300 can provide 150 face masks to refugee health workers on the front lines. With your support, we can continue our work to keep children in the GAZA and around the world healthy, educated
